The Crew

MEET THE
OPERATIVES

Six distinct personalities. One shared obsession — making you look like your best self.

N
Senior Barber
NEDAS
Always sharp, always on point. Nedas gets the details right every time.

Nedas. An OG from Šiauliai. A true punk. Founder of Cyber Punk Barbers.

Cyber Punk is in his blood. From the very first brick, from the very first chair. Not just a studio — a place with character. Just like him.

He’s been cutting since the world started cracking — since 2008, when the crisis wiped suits off office desks. Instead of a manager’s chair — scissors in hand. And he never stopped. 17 years behind the chair.

If you haven’t been to him yet — you haven’t been to a real barber. Nedas wastes neither your time nor his words. A haircut is like a hit: fast, precise, with character. Conversations — if there’s something worth saying. Silence — if it’s better that way. Maybe a swear word or two. After the cut, he usually hears just one word: “F*cking good.”

In his chair, there’s one unwritten rule: “The devil is in the details. Or God.”

If you happen to fall asleep — don’t worry. He’ll keep cutting. And he’ll do it well.

Come through. Try it. The time to change is now.

Book with Nedas
D
Style Engineer
DENIS
Brings the energy and the finest fades. Denis knows exactly what suits you.

@denio.laboratory – instead of balancing wheels, he chose working with heads.

Denis left the garage and realized that a barber’s chair was closer to his heart. He used to be an auto mechanic, but one day, after an argument with his boss, while smoking outside, he remembered how, at fifteen, he used to cut his grandfather’s and brother’s hair. That same evening, he called a barbershop, told his story—and they took him in.

His first chair was on Gediminas Avenue—there he was taught by a Ukrainian barber. Later, he met Neda, the “gangster” of our barbershop, who became a true mentor.

And that’s how it started—7 years behind the chair.

Today, he says barbering is not a job, but a way of life. He sees himself cutting hair for the rest of his life. There are no limits here, just like in relationships—every person is unique, always different. Over these years, Denis has matured: he’s learned a lot about people, about humanity, about how respect and listening are not tricks, but essential tools—just as important as scissors.

His signature? Charisma. That unteachable spark—you either have it or you don’t. He comes to work to bring a good mood, and expects the same from others. He calls his philosophy “synthesis”—an exchange of emotion for emotion. Problems don’t enter the chair; they stay outside the door.

After a haircut, clients usually say a simple “thank you.” And that’s enough for him. Because it’s not just about clean lines or a perfect fade—it’s about connection, atmosphere, about how, for an hour, you and he become like-minded.

He’s grateful to those who follow him, travel with him, grow with him. Because in barbering, he says, just like in life—there’s no perfection, only the journey.

One sentence for those who haven’t visited Denis yet:
“This is not just a haircut—this is synthesis.”

E
Precision Specialist
ERIKAS
Clean lines and classic cuts. Erikas executes the craft with pure focus.

Erikas. Not a saint, not an artist — just a guy with clippers in his hand. Cutting for 2.5 years now. Not because it was a dream, but because he needed money. He was searching for himself, found scissors, clippers, and his place among people who don’t talk too much.

Over those years, he’s changed as much as someone changes when they see different faces every day but remain the same inside — quiet, calm, not a morning person. He doesn’t like rules. His style is simple: no unnecessary tricks, just a clean line and freedom. What sets him apart is that he doesn’t try to please anyone. And maybe that’s exactly why people come back.

What drives him the most? Colleagues, freedom, that calm between haircuts — when you can take a smoke and laugh about life being here and now. He’s not into music, but if something’s playing, it might be Lady Gaga — why not, the world is absurd anyway.

Clients? Everyone’s welcome. If you fall asleep in the chair — he won’t wake you. If you don’t talk — even better. After the cut, people usually don’t say much. Why would they? The mirror says everything.

In his free time, he sells furniture, likes to travel, but nothing really stresses him — the world is on fire, and he’s chill about it. Coffee instead of beer. Bolt Drive instead of Audi or BMW.

One sentence for those who haven’t been to him yet:
“Too expensive.”

And one more unwritten rule: when you sit in his chair, you understand — there are no rules here.

Book with Erikas
V
Detail Master
VLAD
Quiet precision. Vlad lets the results speak for themselves.

Vlad. @vlad.arh. The one who got into barbering for the vibe.

Clients don’t come just for the fade. They come back for the conversation, for the atmosphere. Over time, familiar faces turn into friends — they say hello, share how their trip went, and leave with a “f*cking good” or “amazing, as always.”

Vlad likes open and bold people. The ones who say what they like — and what they don’t. Criticism doesn’t offend here — it’s the path to a better cut, to growth.

He pulls up to work in a Mercedes. In the shop, he throws on minimal house or techno, takes a sip of his Monster — and that’s it: let’s go, time to cut.

Ask him what he’d do if a client fell asleep, and his answer is simple:
“What do you mean what — I’d keep cutting. Let him sleep. Full-on Relax FM vibe.”

Book with Vlad
M
Creative Lead
MARTYNAS
Next-level scissor work. Martynas turns every cut into a masterpiece.
Martynas. @martynas_balaika

Five years behind the chair — and still carrying the same vibe.

He didn’t come into barbering just for a job — he came for the energy.
When he moved to Vilnius, he walked into a barbershop and felt it immediately:
there were no strict rules here, just freedom. That’s when he decided — this is my place.

He completed training at Barber Academy and Red Light Barbers, and now creates his own rhythm — blending hip-hop, trap, and UK drill energy into his work and lifestyle.

When he sends a client off with a smile and says:
“Today you’re gonna get that ;)” — he knows it’s done right.

After a haircut, the most common word you’ll hear is:
“Clean.”

In his chair, there are no strict rules.
If you fall asleep, you’ll hear: “Bro, wake up.”
If it’s your first time — just know, this is not a regular beauty salon.

Between cuts: herbal tea, beer, laughter.
Between thoughts: a dream of owning his own barbershop one day.

His style is freedom, music, and good energy.

It’s gonna be legit, bro. Always.
Book with Martynas
E
Classic Specialist
EVALDAS
Old school craft meets modern edge. Good vibes guaranteed.

Evaldas. @aktyvus

The youngest in experience, but already shaped by life.

Just half a year ago, he didn’t know how to hold scissors. Today, he shapes heads as if they were blocks of marble. For him, every haircut is a sculpture — and tools are an extension of his hands.

He entered barbering searching for freedom — a space for creativity, growth, and constant learning. And he found it. Today, he is a DJ, yoga teacher, and barber in one — three different worlds coming together into one flow: music, rhythm, and precise movement.

In Evaldas’ chair, there are no strict rules — only respect, listening, and pure vibe.
His ideal client? Anyone who has hair.

If you fall asleep, he won’t wake you — he’ll gently make you comfortable so you can rest peacefully.

Coffee flows like fuel here — strong enough to ring in your ears. Between cuts, you might hear the crackle of “Golden Virginia” tobacco. In the background, aggressive acid music may be playing — his creative soundtrack.

And if you haven’t been to Evaldas yet, remember this:
you don’t just come for a haircut. You enter his space. And there, your head becomes a canvas — a sculpture.
